Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a great breakfast option,it gives you energy to start your day

Ingredients

  • Grounded oats
  • Milk
  • pinch of salt
  • Peanut butter
  • honey
  • Any fruit

Steps

  1. Mix together oats,milk,salt
  2. Put in microwave on high temp for 2 minutes
  3. Add peanut butter,honey and fruit you chose
  4. Enjoy
