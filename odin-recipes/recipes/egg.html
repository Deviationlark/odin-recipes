Egg salad

Description

Egg salad is a dish that is eaten on the side preferably with meat

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Make a basic salad with cucumber,lettuce,onion
  2. Boil an egg
  3. Chop the egg in pieces
  4. Add egg in salad
  5. Enjoy
